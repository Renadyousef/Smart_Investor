The Model looks at the Previous 60 trading days
Then predicts Next trading day's closing price

*******Using the model will be like this************
X = last 60 days WE FETCH IT LIVE FROM SAUDI MARKET
y = day 61 THE MODEL PREDICTS IT

--- DEV Notes
The training data was scaled using:

scaler = MinMaxScaler(feature_range=(0, 1))

So after prediction you should convert it back:

pred_scaled = predict_next_day(last_60_days_scaled)

pred_price = scaler.inverse_transform(
    [[pred_scaled]]
)[0][0]

buy/sell logic
Choose stock
      ↓
Load corresponding model
      ↓
Fetch latest prices
      ↓
Take last 60 days
      ↓
Predict next day's closing price
      ↓
Convert back to SAR
      ↓
Compare with today's price
      ↓
BUY / HOLD / SELL
